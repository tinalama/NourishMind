Anorexia Nervosa

An intense fear of gaining weight, leading to extreme food restriction and a distorted body image.

Bulimia Nervosa

A cycle of binge eating followed by compensatory behaviors like vomiting or excessive exercise to avoid weight gain.

Binge Eating Disorder (BED)

Recurrent episodes of eating large quantities of food without purging behaviors, often leading to feelings of guilt or distress.

Avoidant / Restrictive Food intake Disorder (ARFID)

Avoiding certain foods or limiting intake due to sensory issues or fear of negative consequences, not related to body image.
